Do you work with businesses outside Canada?
Yes — we work globally. Most of our infrastructure clients are in North America, the UAE, Europe, and Asia Pacific. All communication is remote and asynchronous. Billing in CAD, USD, or crypto accepted.
How do I pay?
Wire transfer, Stripe (CAD/USD), Interac e-Transfer (Canadian clients), Bitcoin, or Ethereum. 50% upfront, 50% on delivery for Starter and Growth. Enterprise and Empire invoiced monthly or by milestone.
Can I start with Starter and upgrade later?
Yes — and that's common. Many clients start with a landing page and upgrade to Growth when they're ready for full infrastructure. We build with upgrade paths in mind from day one.
What's VIGILAX and do I need it?
VIGILAX is Francisco Holdings' proprietary AI cybersecurity framework — constitutional AI boundary enforcement, prompt injection prevention, and threat detection. Enterprise and regulated industries benefit most. Starter and Growth clients can add it as an upgrade.
Do you provide ongoing maintenance?
Yes. Post-launch support is included per tier (1–90 days). After that, monthly maintenance retainers start at $299 CAD/mo for Starter sites and $999/mo for Growth and above. Empire clients have ongoing management as part of the tier.
What makes Francisco Holdings different from a regular agency?
We built 45+ companies ourselves — we didn't just design for clients. We know what breaks under real traffic, real payments, and real legal scrutiny. We also bring BENO-X constitutional frameworks and AI security at no extra charge on Enterprise and Empire tiers.
How fast can you really start?
Same business day for Growth and above. We confirm scope, you send 50% deposit, we begin. Starter clients get started within 48 hours. No weeks-long onboarding. No bloated agency process.
