The Story

Built on Real Iron

"There are cars, and then there are machines that carry history in their steel. I only deal in the second kind."

KOOLDUCE MOTORS was born from a personal obsession: acquiring the rarest, most authentic classic vehicles Ontario has to offer, and connecting them with collectors who truly understand their worth.

The name comes from a real car — a 1929 Ford rat rod, custom-built, matte black, red interior, white walls. The plate reads KOOLDUCE. It's not a replica. It's not a weekend project. It's a statement.

Alongside it: a museum-quality 1930 Ford Model A Coupe with its original Boyce MotoMeter still on the hood. A 1970 Chevelle SS in Cranberry Red that draws a crowd every time it rolls. These aren't cars you find at a dealership. These are cars that find you.

We're actively growing. Supercars. Trucks. Sport bikes. Anything with provenance, authenticity, and a story worth telling. If you're serious about collector vehicles — in any category — you're in the right place.
