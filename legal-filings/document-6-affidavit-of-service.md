## FILING INSTRUCTIONS

1. Print this document (remove the markdown header/instructions before printing)
2. Fill in all [BRACKETED] placeholders with your actual information
3. Bring to a Commissioner for Taking Affidavits (any Ontario lawyer's office, many banks, or the court registrar's office) — bring government ID
4. Swear/affirm in front of the Commissioner — do NOT sign before you appear before them
5. Commissioner signs and stamps
6. File the original with the court registrar (Kawartha Lakes Superior Court, 180 Kent St W, Lindsay ON K9V 2Z8)
7. Keep a certified copy for your records

**Cost:** Court filing fee for affidavit — check current tariff at Ontario Ministry of the Attorney General website.
