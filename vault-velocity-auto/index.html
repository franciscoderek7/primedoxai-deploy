Questions

Frequently Asked

Are deposits refundable?
Yes, 100%. If we cannot source your target vehicle or if you choose not to proceed after receiving full vehicle details, your $50,000 deposit is returned in full within 5 business days.
How do I verify the car's history?
We provide full Carfax report, UAE registration history, service records, seizure/estate documentation, and our own pre-sale inspection report. Every vehicle sold with full provenance.
Can I visit the Dubai garage?
Yes — by appointment only. Buyers who have placed a deposit may visit our Dubai facility to inspect their vehicle mid-restoration or at completion. Contact us to arrange.
Do you accept trade-ins?
Yes. We accept exotic and luxury vehicles as partial trade against a purchase. Vehicle must be appraised by our team. Contact us with details and photos of your trade-in.
What warranty do you offer?
All restored vehicles include a 12-month / 20,000km drivetrain warranty. This covers engine, transmission, and all mechanical systems rebuilt by Dylan's Garage. Cosmetics and electronics are covered for 90 days.
How long does restoration take?
3 to 6 months depending on extent of restoration required. Vehicles listed as "Available" have been fully restored and are ready for delivery. "In Restoration" vehicles have a quoted completion timeline.
Do you export to Canada?
Yes, Canada is our primary export market. We handle all Transport Canada compliance, RIV import paperwork, and provincial registration support. Duties are the buyer's responsibility and we provide guidance on calculations.
What payment methods do you accept?
Wire transfer (USD, CAD, AED), Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Stripe credit card (coming soon for deposits). All transactions are documented with legal purchase agreements.
