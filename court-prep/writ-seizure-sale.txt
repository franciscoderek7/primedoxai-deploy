HOW TO ENFORCE — STEPS FOR DEREK:

1. Obtain judgment first (file Statement of Claim →
   proceed through court → obtain judgment order)
2. Get certified copy of judgment from court registrar
3. Bring to court to have Writ issued (small fee)
4. File Writ with Sheriff's office in county where
   debtor's assets are located (pay advance fee ~$150-500)
5. Sheriff seizes and sells debtor's assets
6. Proceeds paid to you after Sheriff's fees deducted
