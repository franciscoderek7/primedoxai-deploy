HOW TO OBTAIN A CPL — STEPS FOR DEREK:

1. File your Statement of Claim first (get file number)
2. Bring a motion (notice of motion + affidavit) to the
   court requesting the CPL be issued
3. The judge or registrar reviews and signs the CPL
4. File the signed CPL at the Land Registry Office
   for the county where the property is located
5. Pay the Land Registry filing fee (approx. $70-$100)
6. The CPL is now on title — defendant CANNOT sell or
   mortgage the property without satisfying your claim first
