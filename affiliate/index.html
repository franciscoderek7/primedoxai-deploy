Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before applying.

How is my referral tracked? ▾
Your unique referral code is added as a URL parameter (e.g. ?ref=YOURCODE) on all product links. When a customer clicks your link and purchases within 30 days, the sale is attributed to you.
When and how do I get paid? ▾
Payouts are processed on the 1st of every month for the prior month's commissions. Minimum payout is $50 CAD. Choose PayPal or Interac e-Transfer when you apply.
Is the 20% rate recurring for subscriptions? ▾
Yes — for monthly subscription products (PrimeDox AI, OmniaGuard, CleanSwarm, FH Retainer), you earn 20% every month the customer stays subscribed. Lifetime recurring commission.
Can I promote all products at once? ▾
Yes. One referral link per product, all managed under your single affiliate account. You'll receive tracking links for each product on approval.
Do I need to be in Canada? ▾
No. Affiliates can be anywhere in the world. However, CCLDR and Weedlaw Education are specifically relevant to Canadian audiences (Canadian Charter of Rights).
Is there an affiliate dashboard? ▾
Currently we operate a manual tracking system. You'll receive a monthly commission statement by email on the 28th of each month showing your referrals and payout amount. A self-serve dashboard is coming in Q3 2026.
