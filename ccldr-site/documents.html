A printable wallet-sized card with the 6 critical steps for any cannabis arrest in Canada. Covers s.7 silence, s.10(b) counsel, and s.8 non-consent.
KNOW YOUR RIGHTS — Cannabis Arrest Protocol CCLDR.NET | Education Only — Not Legal Advice 1. STAY CALM. Do not resist physically. 2. "I am exercising my right to remain silent." (s.7 Charter) 3. "I wish to speak to a lawyer." (s.10(b) Charter) — Then STOP TALKING. 4. "I do not consent to this search." (s.8 Charter) 5. Note badge numbers, time, location, witnesses.
