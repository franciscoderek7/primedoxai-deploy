Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the partner program.

Do I need to be a lawyer to join?
No. The CCLDR Partner Program is open to lawyers, paralegals, legal aid workers, cannabis advocates, community organizers, and anyone who works with Canadians navigating the justice system. You do not need a law license to participate.
When do I get paid?
Commissions are paid monthly via Interac e-Transfer, typically within the first week of the month for the previous month's earned commissions. You'll receive a payment summary with each transfer.
Is there a minimum commission threshold?
No minimum. Even a single referred client at the Foundation tier earns you $37.25/month. There is no payout floor — you earn from the first referral.
How long do I earn commission on a referred client?
For as long as they remain subscribed to CCLDR.NET. Commission is lifetime per referred client — meaning one successful referral can generate recurring monthly income indefinitely.
What am I actually referring clients to?
CCLDR.NET is a cannabis constitutional law education platform. It provides access to 3,000+ legal resources, 350+ court-ready document templates, AI-powered consultation, and 20+ years of Derek Francisco's constitutional defense knowledge. All content is educational only — not legal advice.

This is an educational referral program. No legal services are provided by CCLDR.NET or Derek Francisco. Partners are referring clients to an educational platform only. No referral constitutes a recommendation of legal services. Derek Francisco is not a licensed attorney.
