Our Mission

What we're fighting for.

  • ⚖️ Full legalization of cannabis cultivation for personal use — the Parker principle upheld
  • 🛡️ Charter s.7 medical necessity as a complete defense for all cannabis charges
  • 📋 Mandatory Crown disclosure — no more surprise prosecutions
  • 🏥 Recognition of cannabis as medicine for all conditions — not just authorized ones
  • 📚 Free, accessible legal education for all Canadians charged under the Cannabis Act
  • 💰 Restitution for all Canadians wrongfully convicted of cannabis offenses pre-legalization
