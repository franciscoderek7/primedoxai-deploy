Demand Forecasting

AI demand sensing that integrates POS data, macroeconomic signals, social sentiment, weather patterns, and promotional calendars to generate SKU-level forecasts 30/60/90 days ahead. Clients using Logistics Swarm demand forecasting reduce inventory write-offs by 41% and stockouts by 67% — simultaneously freeing $3.4M average working capital per $100M revenue.

Inventory AI

Autonomous inventory positioning engine that determines optimal safety stock levels, reorder points, and allocation across your distribution network. Carrier Selection AI

Real-time carrier selection and rate negotiation engine analyzing 400+ carrier performance metrics, real-time capacity availability, lane pricing, and service level history.
