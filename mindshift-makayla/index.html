心 Kokoro — Heart · Mind · Spirit
Philosophy

Kokoro No Tenkan

In Japanese, kokoro (心) is richer than any single English word — it is the seat of consciousness, feeling, and will. Tenkan (転換) is the decisive pivot, the turning point. Together: the transformation of the whole self.

As a fourth-year psychology student, I've spent years studying the research behind how minds change — neuroplasticity, behavioral science, positive psychology. I bring that knowledge into my coaching as tools I'm passionate about, not clinical expertise I've claimed.

This is honest growth work. Grounded in peer-reviewed research I've studied. Delivered with the energy of someone who genuinely believes you can shift your story.

✓ Research-informed · Peer-reviewed frameworks · Psychology student perspective
