The Philosophy

"The most powerful revolution
doesn't happen in the streets —
it happens in the silence of a single mind
choosing to transform."

心の転換 — Kokoro No Tenkan — means the transformation of heart and mind. It is the ancient Japanese understanding that lasting change begins within, radiates outward, and ultimately reshapes the world. This is MindShift.

Evidence-informed mindset work grounded in four years of university-level psychological science. The science of how minds change — applied to real people, real lives.

Makayla Francisco completed her fourth year of a Bachelor of Science in Psychology at age 21 — one of the youngest in her cohort. She is a trained mindset coach, a future published author, and the founder of MindShift, a company she built on one belief: the planet heals when people do.
