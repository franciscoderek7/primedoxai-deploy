What We Do

Compliance Services

Comprehensive regulatory support across the full compliance lifecycle — from initial application through ongoing audit readiness.

📋

Licence Application Support

Navigate the Health Canada cannabis licence application process with structured support, document preparation, and submission review.

📁

SOP Development

Develop compliant Standard Operating Procedures across cultivation, processing, security, packaging, and quality assurance.

🗃

CTS Record-Keeping

Cannabis Tracking System compliance — set up proper reporting workflows, inventory reconciliation, and regulatory submissions.

Regulatory Audit Prep

Pre-audit assessments, gap remediation, and mock inspections to ensure you're ready for Health Canada compliance reviews.

Municipal Zoning

Navigate municipal zoning requirements, setback rules, and land-use approvals for cannabis retail and production facilities.

🔐

Security Plan Design

Develop compliant security plans meeting Cannabis Regulations requirements — physical controls, CCTV, intrusion detection, and access management.
