The Host

Meet Derek Francisco

DJ
Derek Francisco
@DocWeedlaw

Derek Francisco is a constitutional research student with over 15 years of focused study on Canadian cannabis law, Charter jurisprudence, and regulatory framework analysis. Based in Lindsay, Ontario, Derek created the BENO-X Framework (Bad Exemption = No Offense) and has developed educational content covering landmark cases including R v Parker (2000), R v Smith (2015 SCC 34), and Allard v Canada (2016 FC 236).

Derek built Doc Weedlaw Media to make complex constitutional concepts accessible to everyday Canadians — patients, veterans, growers, and advocates who deserve to understand the legal landscape around cannabis.

⚠️ NOT a lawyer — a dedicated constitutional student
