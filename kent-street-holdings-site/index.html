About

Founded on Kent Street

Kent Street Holdings manages real estate assets for the Francisco Holdings group. Based on Kent Street West, Lindsay, Ontario — the founding address of the Francisco Holdings empire — we operate with deep roots in the Kawartha Lakes market and a long-term perspective on Ontario's secondary real estate markets.


We are operators, not speculators. Our focus is income-producing property, responsible management, and building long-term value in the communities where we operate. Lindsay and the Kawartha Lakes region represent a fundamentally undervalued market — and we are building our position carefully and deliberately.
