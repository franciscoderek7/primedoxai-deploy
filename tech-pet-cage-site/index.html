About Us

Local. Reliable. Hands-On.

Tech Pet Cage is a registered Ontario business based in Lindsay, serving the Kawartha Lakes region and beyond. We combine technical expertise with a passion for animals to offer a unique range of products and services under one roof.

Founded by Derek Francisco, Tech Pet Cage is the operational base for a growing portfolio of projects — from neighbourhood tech repair to custom cage fabrication. We believe in doing quality work, treating every customer fairly, and building something real.

Whether you need your device repaired, the right food for your pet, or a custom enclosure built exactly to spec — we get it done.
