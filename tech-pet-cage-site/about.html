To make premium smart pet technology accessible to every pet owner on the planet — by sourcing directly from the world's best suppliers, removing retail markups, and passing the savings straight to you. We're not just a store. We're a curation engine for the future of pet care.

Derek's Story

Derek Francisco is a technology entrepreneur who has spent the better part of a decade building digital businesses across multiple verticals. As the founder of Francisco Holdings Inc., a growing portfolio of online brands, Derek has always believed that the best technology should be accessible to everyone — not just those who can afford premium retail prices.

"I got tired of paying $300 for a pet camera I found for $60 at the source. So I built TechPetCage to fix that — for every pet parent."

When his rescue dog Luna struggled with meal times during long work days, Derek started researching smart feeders. He was shocked to find that the same products sold for 2-3x more through traditional retail. He spent months sourcing directly, vetting suppliers, and testing products — and TechPetCage was born.

Today, TechPetCage is part of the Francisco Holdings Inc. portfolio — a collection of direct-to-consumer brands built on the same principles: transparency, quality, and value. Derek is personally involved in every product decision.

Why Dropshipping?

We believe in being transparent about how we work — and why it benefits you

💰

Lower Prices

By shipping directly from our global supplier network, we eliminate warehousing costs, storage fees, and retail markup layers. You get the same product for significantly less — often 30-50% below traditional retail prices.

🛡️

Curated Quality

We don't list every product we find. Every item on TechPetCage must have at least a 4.5-star rating and 500+ verified orders before it earns a place in our store. We test the best, skip the rest.

🌍

Global Reach

Our supplier network spans the globe, which means we can offer worldwide shipping to virtually any country. No matter where you and your pet call home, smart pet tech can reach you.
