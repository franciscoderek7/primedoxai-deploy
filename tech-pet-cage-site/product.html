About the AutoFeed WiFi Pro

The AutoFeed WiFi Pro is the smart pet feeder your pets have been waiting for. Powered by the latest WiFi connectivity, it puts complete control of your pet's feeding schedule right on your smartphone — anytime, anywhere.

With a generous 6-liter hopper (that's roughly a 45-day supply for most cats), it's built for pet owners who travel frequently or have demanding schedules. The precision portion control ensures your pet gets exactly the right amount — no overfeeding, no missed meals.

The integrated HD camera lets you watch your pet eat in real time, and the two-way audio with voice recording means you can record a personal message so your pet hears your familiar voice at feeding time — reducing feeding anxiety for nervous animals.
