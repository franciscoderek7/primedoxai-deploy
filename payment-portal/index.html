🍁 Interac e-Transfer — Step by Step

Interac e-Transfer is the easiest way for Canadians to pay — directly from your bank app, no card needed, no processing fees. Follow these steps to complete your purchase.

  1. Choose your product — decide which product and tier you want (see price table below). Note the exact product name and price.
  2. Open your bank's app or website — look for "Send Interac e-Transfer" (most Canadian banks: RBC, TD, BMO, Scotiabank, CIBC, Tangerine, PC Financial, EQ Bank all support this).
  3. Send to this email address:
    derek@franciscoholdings.com
    Auto-deposit is enabled — your transfer arrives instantly without needing a security question/answer. If your bank requires a security question anyway, use any question and the answer: francisco2026
  4. Enter the exact CAD amount for the product you're purchasing (see table below). Use the exact price — do not round.
  5. In the message/memo field, write:
    [PRODUCT NAME] — [YOUR EMAIL]
    Example: CCLDR Warrior — yourname@email.com
  6. Send your transfer — then email derek@franciscoholdings.com with:
    • Subject: "Interac Payment — [Product]"
    • Your name and email
    • Product purchased + amount sent
    • Reference/confirmation number from your bank (optional but helpful)
  7. Access granted within 2 hours — Derek manually verifies and activates your account. For CCLDR, you'll receive login credentials by email. For subscriptions (CleanSwarm, OmniaGuard, PrimeDox), you'll receive a Stripe subscription link to set up recurring billing after the first month.

❓ Payment FAQs
Will I be charged in CAD or USD?+

All prices are in Canadian dollars (CAD). If your card is USD or another currency, your bank converts at their exchange rate. To avoid conversion, Canadian customers can use Interac e-Transfer (exact CAD amount, no conversion).

Is it safe to enter my card on your checkout?+

Yes. Our checkout pages are hosted by Stripe — the payment processor used by Amazon, Shopify, and millions of other businesses. Your card details go directly to Stripe's servers; we never see or store your card number. Stripe is PCI-DSS Level 1 certified — the highest security standard for card data.

Can I pay in monthly installments?+

Monthly payment plans are available for CCLDR Elite ($999) and Sovereign ($1,499) tiers on request — 3-month installment plans available. Email derek@franciscoholdings.com with "Installment Plan — [Tier]" to arrange. Interac e-Transfer only for installment plans.

What's the refund policy?+

Subscriptions (CleanSwarm, OmniaGuard, PrimeDox): 7-day money-back if fewer than 5 logins. CCLDR one-time tiers: 14-day refund if fewer than 20% of content accessed. Email derek@franciscoholdings.com — "Refund Request — [Product] — [Order #]". Processed within 3 business days.

I sent an Interac e-Transfer but haven't received access yet+

Interac transfers take up to 30 minutes to appear (some banks are instant). Once received, access is granted within 2 hours. If it's been more than 2 hours after your bank confirms delivery: message Telegram @OmniguardSec_bot or email derek@franciscoholdings.com with your transfer confirmation number. Note: transfers sent to the wrong email address cannot be retrieved by us — always double-check the address before sending.

Do you offer bulk / group pricing?+

Yes. Group pricing is available for: CCLDR institutional licenses (paralegal firms, law offices, community organizations), CleanSwarm multi-location bundles, OmniaGuard multi-site enterprise contracts. Email derek@franciscoholdings.com with your organization name, product needed, and number of seats/locations.
