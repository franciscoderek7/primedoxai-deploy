Frequently Asked Questions — All Products

🧹 CLEANSWARM
How do I add my first crew member?+

Log in to your CleanSwarm dashboard → Crew → Add Crew Member. Enter their name, mobile number, and skill tags. They'll receive a SMS invite to download the CleanSwarm Crew app. You can also import crew from a CSV file via Settings → Crew → Bulk Import.

How does route optimization work?+

Enable it at Settings → Dispatch → Route Optimization → Enable. CleanSwarm's AI analyzes job locations, crew locations, traffic patterns, and time windows to build the most fuel-efficient routes daily. Average fuel savings: 34%. The system recalculates in real-time if a job is added, cancelled, or rescheduled.

Can I customize invoice templates?+

Yes. Go to Settings → Invoicing → Templates. You can add your logo, customize colors, add custom line items, and set default payment terms (Net 7, Net 14, Net 30). PDF invoices include a QR code linking to the online payment portal. E-transfer instructions are auto-appended if you enable it under Payment Methods.

What's the difference between Starter and Growth?+
  • Starter ($199/mo): Up to 5 crew members, basic dispatch, auto-invoicing, route optimization, review collection
  • Growth ($499/mo): Up to 15 crew, multi-location dispatch, AI revenue forecasting, white-label client portal, priority support
  • Scale ($1,249/mo): Unlimited crew, custom integrations, dedicated account manager, API access, white-label mobile app

You can upgrade anytime — billing is prorated from the upgrade date.

My crew member isn't receiving job notifications — why?+

Check: 1) The crew member has the CleanSwarm app installed and notifications enabled. 2) Their phone number is correct (Settings → Crew → [Name] → Edit). 3) They're not marked "Unavailable" on the schedule. If notifications still don't arrive within 5 minutes of dispatch, ask them to log out and back in. For persistent issues, contact support via Telegram @OmniguardSec_bot.

What is CCLDR and is it legal advice?+

Nothing on CCLDR constitutes legal representation or establishes a solicitor-client relationship. Always retain qualified legal counsel for your specific legal matter.

What is the BENO-X Framework?+

BENO-X = Bad Exemption, No Offense. It's a constitutional analysis methodology developed from studying Canadian cannabis case law. The framework examines three questions: 1) Does an exemption mechanism exist? 2) Is that exemption functional and accessible? 3) What is the constitutional consequence of a defective exemption? It draws on R v Parker (2000), R v Smith (2015 SCC 34), and R v Allard (2016 FC 236). This is educational analysis — not legal advice for any specific case.

How long does initial deployment take?+

OmniaGuard deploys within 24–72 hours for most environments. The process: Day 1 — credential-free network scan and asset inventory. Day 2 — threat model calibration. Day 3 — AI monitoring goes live. For enterprise environments with 500+ endpoints, deployment is 5–10 business days. Your dedicated security engineer is available on Telegram @OmniguardSec_bot from Day 1.

What compliance frameworks does OmniaGuard support?+

  • ISO 42001 (AI Management Systems)
  • SOC 2 Type II readiness reporting
  • PIPEDA / Bill C-27 data protection controls
  • Health Canada LP security requirements

Compliance reports are generated automatically and available for download from your dashboard under Reports → Compliance.

What happens when a threat is detected?+

OmniaGuard uses a 4-level response protocol: Level 1 (Log) — anomaly logged, no action. Level 2 (Alert) — push notification + email to your security team within 30 seconds. Level 3 (Contain) — automatic network segmentation of affected endpoint, alert escalated. Level 4 (Critical) — automatic isolation + immediate Telegram alert to you + OmniaGuard SOC analyst paged. All Level 3+ events are reviewed by a human analyst within 15 minutes.

Can OmniaGuard protect a cannabis LP operation?+

Yes. OmniaGuard has specialized modules for licensed producers including CTS (Cannabis Tracking System) data protection, seed-to-sale audit log integrity monitoring, health Canada reporting security, and physical/digital convergence monitoring. Cannabis clients are among our fastest-growing segment. Contact @OmniguardSec_bot for an LP-specific security assessment.

🤖 PRIMEDOX AI
What does PrimeDox AI actually do?+

PrimeDox AI is an AI-assisted document intelligence platform. It helps you organize, analyze, and understand complex documents — particularly legal, regulatory, and compliance documents. Features include: automated document classification, key clause extraction, cross-reference analysis, plain-language summaries, and the BENO-X constitutional analysis assistant. PrimeDox AI is an educational and productivity tool — NOT a licensed legal service.

What file types does PrimeDox accept?+
  • PDF (including scanned documents with OCR)
  • DOCX / DOC (Microsoft Word)
  • TXT, RTF, HTML
  • Images (JPG, PNG) via OCR — Pro and Elite tiers only

File size limit: 50MB per document / 200MB per batch upload (Elite tier). Storage: 5GB (Pro) / Unlimited (Elite).

Is my data private and secure?+

Yes. PrimeDox AI stores documents encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Documents are processed in an isolated compute environment — your documents are never used to train AI models. Data is stored on Canadian servers (AWS Canada Central, Montreal). You own your data; you can export or delete at any time. PrimeDox does not share your data with third parties. Privacy policy: primedoxai.ca/privacy.
