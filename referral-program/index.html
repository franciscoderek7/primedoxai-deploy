Program Terms
  • Commission rate: 20% of monthly subscription, recurring
  • Tracking: Stripe metadata + UTM parameters (?ref=yourname)
  • Attribution window: First click wins; 30-day cookie
  • Payout: Monthly via Interac e-Transfer on the 1st of each month
  • Minimum payout: $25 CAD (commissions roll over until threshold reached)
  • No commission on refunded transactions
  • No self-referrals
