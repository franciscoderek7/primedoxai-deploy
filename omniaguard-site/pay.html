Send to: contact@omniaguard.com
  1. 1. Open your online banking app → Interac e-Transfer → Send Money
  2. 2. Enter contact@omniaguard.com as the recipient
  3. 3. Set amount to your tier's monthly fee (CAD)
  4. 4. In the message field, write: OmniaGuard [Tier Name] — [Your Company]
  5. 5. After sending, email contact@omniaguard.com with your receipt screenshot
  6. 6. Expect access credentials within 4 hours of confirmed payment
