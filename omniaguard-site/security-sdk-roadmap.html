SR&ED Funding Opportunity

Scientific Research & Experimental Development — 35% Federal Tax Credit (Canada)

OmniaGuard's SDK development work qualifies for SR&ED tax credits under the Government of Canada's program for technology innovation. Both Phase 2 and Phase 3 contain significant eligible experimental development activity.

Phase 2 Eligible Amount
$52,500–$87,500
35% federal refundable tax credit on $150K–$250K eligible expenditure
Phase 3 Eligible Amount
$350K–$700K
35% refundable on $1M–$2M; may combine with provincial credits for higher effective rate
Application Timeline
Annual
File T661 form with annual corporate tax return. 18-month filing window after fiscal year end.
IRAP Eligibility
Up to $50K
NRC IRAP can fund Phase 2 technical activities. Apply 6–12 months before project start.
How to apply: SR&ED claims are filed with the T661 form as part of your corporate tax return. Contact the CRA SR&ED Program or engage a qualified SR&ED consultant to maximize eligible claims.
