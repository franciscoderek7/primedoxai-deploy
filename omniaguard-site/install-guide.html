Install OmniaGuard on Your Device

OmniaGuard installs as a Progressive Web App — no app store required. Full offline access, push alerts, and standalone mode on any device.

📱
Detected: Detecting... — Follow the highlighted instructions below.
Android (Chrome)
1

Open Chrome browser

Open Google Chrome on your Android device and navigate to omniaguard.com. Chrome is required — other browsers may not support full PWA installation.

2

Tap the 3-dot menu

Tap the three vertical dots (⋮) in the top-right corner of Chrome to open the browser menu.

3

Tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App"

Look for Add to Home Screen or Install App in the menu. This option appears when the site is PWA-ready. On newer Chrome versions it may say "Install OmniaGuard".

4

Confirm the installation

Tap Install in the popup dialog. Chrome may ask for confirmation. Tap Install again to confirm.

5

Launch from Home Screen

Find the OmniaGuard icon on your Android home screen. Tap it to open OmniaGuard in standalone mode — no browser chrome, full screen, offline-capable.

⚠️
Samsung Galaxy Tab (Android) Note: On Samsung devices with Samsung Internet, look for the Install option in the browser banner that appears at the bottom of the screen. Alternatively, tap the menu icon and select "Add page to" → "Home screen".
iOS — Safari Required
⚠️ Important: On iPhone and iPad, PWA installation is only supported in Safari. Chrome on iOS cannot install apps to the home screen.
1

Open Safari

Open the Safari browser on your iPhone or iPad and navigate to omniaguard.com.

2

Tap the Share button

Tap the Share button — the box with an arrow pointing upward — at the bottom of the Safari screen (iPhone) or top-right (iPad).

3

Tap "Add to Home Screen"

Scroll down in the Share sheet and tap Add to Home Screen. You may need to scroll to find it. It has a square icon with a plus sign.

4

Confirm the name and tap "Add"

Safari will show the app name as "OmniaGuard". You can rename it if desired. Tap Add in the top-right corner to confirm.

5

Launch from Home Screen

Find the OmniaGuard icon on your iPhone or iPad home screen. Tap it to open in standalone fullscreen mode without Safari navigation bars.

Desktop — Chrome or Edge
1

Navigate to omniaguard.com

Open Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge and go to omniaguard.com. Both browsers support PWA installation on Windows, macOS, and Linux.

2

Look for the install icon in the address bar

Look for a small install icon (⊕) or computer-with-arrow icon in the right side of the address bar. If you see it, click it to install directly.

3

Or use the browser menu

Click the 3-dot menu (Chrome) or ... menu (Edge) → look for Install OmniaGuard or Apps → Install this site as an app.

4

Click "Install" in the dialog

A dialog will appear asking if you want to install OmniaGuard. Click Install to confirm. The app will open in its own window.

5

OmniaGuard opens as a standalone app

OmniaGuard will open in its own dedicated window with no browser navigation. Find it in your taskbar, Start menu (Windows), or Applications folder (macOS) for quick access.

Why install as an app?

OmniaGuard as a PWA delivers a native-like experience across all platforms.

📵
Offline Access
Full access to Dashboard, PrimeDox, and Charter reference even without internet connection.
⚡
Faster Load
All core assets are cached locally. OmniaGuard loads instantly from the home screen — no waiting.
🏠
Home Screen Shortcut
One tap from your home screen or desktop. No need to open a browser and navigate.
🔔
Push Notifications
Receive threat alerts even when OmniaGuard is not open. (Available in future SDK release.)
⛶
Full-Screen Mode
Runs without browser bars or navigation chrome — full immersive security interface.
🔒
Private & Secure
No app store tracking. No account required. Your data stays on your device.
