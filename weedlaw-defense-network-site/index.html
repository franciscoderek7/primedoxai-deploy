Canadian Charter of Rights — Cannabis Defense Essentials

These constitutional rights apply to every Canadian in every interaction with police.

s.7

Life, Liberty & Security

The right not to be deprived of liberty except in accordance with the principles of fundamental justice. Basis of most constitutional cannabis challenges.

s.8

Unreasonable Search & Seizure

Everyone has the right to be secure against unreasonable search and seizure. Police must have lawful authority and reasonable grounds before searching you or your property.

s.10(b)

Right to Retain Counsel

Upon arrest or detention, you have the right to retain and instruct counsel without delay. Say clearly: "I want to speak with a lawyer." Then remain silent until you do.
