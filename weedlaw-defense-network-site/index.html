Process

How It Works

Three steps from your situation to a qualified professional consultation.

1

Submit Your Situation

Describe your matter using our intake form. We keep your information confidential and use it only to match you with the right network member.

2

Matched With a Member

Our network coordinator reviews your submission and identifies one or more qualified network members who handle your type of matter in your province.

3

Consultation Arranged

You receive a direct introduction to the matched network member. Initial consultation terms are between you and the professional — we facilitate the connection.

Are you a licensed paralegal or cannabis consultant in Canada? Apply to join the Weedlaw Defense Network and receive qualified client referrals.

Membership Requirements

  • Licensed by Law Society where applicable
  • Cannabis specialty or demonstrated experience
  • Good standing with licensing body
  • Errors & omissions insurance current
  • Agreement to Weedlaw Network Code of Ethics

Network membership is reviewed annually. Referrals are provided at no cost to network members. Volume and geographic focus vary.
