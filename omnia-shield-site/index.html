Case Study

Cannabis LP — Ontario

Licensed Producer Secured in 72 Hours — Zero Incidents Since

A mid-sized Ontario licensed cannabis producer approached Omnia Shield following a near-breach event at their primary production facility. Their existing security infrastructure contained critical blind spots in the vault access corridor and secondary employee entry zones — compliance gaps that posed a direct threat to their Health Canada operating license.

Omnia Shield's rapid-deployment team completed a full facility security assessment within the first 24 hours, delivering a comprehensive remediation plan covering CCTV repositioning, access control upgrades, emergency response protocol documentation, and a targeted staff security training session. Full implementation was completed within 72 hours of initial engagement. The producer has recorded zero theft incidents or compliance violations since the Omnia Shield intervention.
