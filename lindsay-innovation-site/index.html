About the Hub

Built for Kawartha Lakes

Based in Lindsay, Ontario, the Lindsay Innovation Hub provides workspace, mentorship, and resources for early-stage technology companies in the Kawartha Lakes region. We believe great innovation doesn't only happen in Toronto or Waterloo — it happens in communities like ours.

From technology startups and digital creatives to cannabis tech, cleaning automation, and IoT ventures — we incubate ideas that matter to our region and beyond.

What We Offer

Programs

Three pathways to take your idea from concept to company.

🏠

Founder Residency

A 12-month residency program for early-stage founders. Includes shared workspace in Lindsay, weekly mentorship, access to our founder network, and introductions to Ontario-based investors and accelerators.

💻

Tech Education

Workshops, bootcamps, and ongoing education for aspiring tech entrepreneurs in the Kawartha Lakes region. Topics include web development, AI tools, digital marketing, and business fundamentals.

🎤

Demo Day

Annual Demo Day bringing together founders, investors, community leaders, and media to showcase innovations built in the Kawartha Lakes region. Open to the public and streamed online.
