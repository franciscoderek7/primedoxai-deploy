Six Pillars of Hospital-Scale AI

Purpose-built clinical AI modules, deployable individually or as a unified hospital intelligence platform.

🧠

Clinical Decision Support AI

Real-time differential diagnosis assistance, evidence-based treatment pathway recommendations, and contraindication alerts integrated directly into existing clinical workflows. Reduce diagnostic errors by up to 89% with specialist-level AI guidance available at every patient encounter.

📊

Patient Risk Stratification

Multi-variable risk scoring engine analyzing vital trends, lab values, medication history, and social determinants of health. Identify at-risk patients 72 hours before clinical deterioration with 97.8% precision, enabling proactive intervention and dramatically reduced ICU transfers.

📋

EHR Optimization

AI-powered clinical documentation that writes discharge summaries, progress notes, and care plans from voice or structured inputs. Reduce documentation burden by 67%, freeing clinicians to spend more time at the bedside and less time at the keyboard.

💳

Claims Processing Automation

End-to-end revenue cycle AI with automated ICD-10/CPT coding validation, claims scrubbing, prior authorization workflows, and denial management. Health networks deploying this module report 94% first-pass claim acceptance rates and 41% reduction in A/R days.

💊

Drug Interaction Monitoring

Real-time polypharmacy analysis with 99.9% detection accuracy across a database of 2.4 million documented drug-drug, drug-disease, and drug-patient interactions. Alerts integrate with order entry systems, pharmacy dispensing, and nursing administration workflows.

🏥

Resource Allocation AI

Predictive bed management, OR scheduling optimization, staff-to-patient ratio forecasting, and supply chain demand planning. Reduce average length of stay by 1.4 days while simultaneously improving patient satisfaction scores and clinician retention rates.
