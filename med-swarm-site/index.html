Platform Capabilities

Built for How Clinics Actually Run

Eight AI-powered modules, deployed individually or as a complete stack. Each one reduces administrative burden and improves care delivery.

📅
Appointment Intelligence

AI fills cancellations automatically. Zero empty slots. MediMind monitors your schedule in real time and matches available patients to open time blocks — without a single phone call.

📋
Patient Intake Automation

AI pre-fills forms from health card data. A 4-minute check-in becomes 45 seconds. Patients confirm, staff verify — the paperwork is already done when they walk in.

💳
OHIP Billing

Direct OHIP integration. Claims submitted in real-time. Zero rejected claims. MediMind validates every service code before submission — eliminating the most costly clerical error in Ontario healthcare.

📄
Medical Record Summary

AI summarizes 50-page charts into 3-paragraph clinical briefings. Physicians see what matters before entering the room — improving consultation quality and reducing review time.

🔗
Referral Management

AI tracks referrals, follows up, and updates status — no calls required. Every specialist referral is monitored from initiation to confirmation, with automated patient communication at each step.

🏠
Home Care Coordination

AI schedules PSWs, nurses, and physiotherapists across Kawartha Lakes. Optimizes routes, manages availability, and handles shift changes — keeping home care networks running without coordinator overhead.

🎥
Telemedicine Suite

Integrated video, AI pre-screening, and follow-up automation. MediMind prepares a structured intake before every virtual visit and sends automated care plans and reminders after.

🔮
Predictive Health Monitoring

AI flags patients at risk before they deteriorate — enabling early intervention. MediMind monitors chronic condition indicators, visit patterns, and lab trends to surface the patients who need attention now.
