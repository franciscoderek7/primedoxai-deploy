Why Privacy Compliance Matters Now

Bill C-27 (CPPA) — Incoming Legislation

Canada's privacy regime is being replaced. Are you ready?

Canada's Bill C-27 (Consumer Privacy Protection Act) is coming. The CPPA will replace PIPEDA with significantly stronger requirements: larger penalties, mandatory breach reporting, enhanced consent standards, and a right to dispose of personal data.

Organizations that build privacy programs now will lead when it becomes law. Those that wait will face mandatory compliance on a compressed timeline — with penalties reaching up to 3% of global annual revenue or $10 million for serious violations.

Start building your privacy program today. Sovereignty Data helps Canadian organizations get compliant with existing obligations and prepared for what's coming.
