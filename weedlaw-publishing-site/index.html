About the Author

Derek Francisco
Doc Weedlaw — Cannabis Law Educator & Author

Derek Francisco, known as Doc Weedlaw, has spent over 15 years researching Canadian cannabis law, constitutional frameworks, and Charter rights as they apply to cannabis patients and the broader community. A self-taught constitutional analyst, Derek developed the BENO-X Framework and 12-Appearance Timeline as practical educational tools for anyone navigating the Canadian cannabis legal landscape.

His writing bridges the gap between complex constitutional theory and practical, accessible education — for patients, advocates, community organizers, and curious citizens alike.
