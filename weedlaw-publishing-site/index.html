About the Author

Derek Francisco

Doc Weedlaw — Cannabis Constitutional Educator

Derek Francisco has spent over 15 years researching the intersection of Canadian cannabis law, the Charter of Rights and Freedoms, and constitutional defense theory. Known as Doc Weedlaw in advocacy circles, Derek developed the BENO-X Framework and the 12-Appearance Timeline — two original analytical tools used by patients, advocates, and self-represented individuals navigating the Canadian legal system.

Through Weedlaw Publishing, Derek makes this research accessible in book and course format — not as legal advice, but as serious educational material for anyone who wants to understand the system they operate within.
