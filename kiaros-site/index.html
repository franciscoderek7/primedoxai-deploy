FAQ

Common Questions

What is Kiaros?
+
Kiaros is an AI intelligence platform built for professionals and enterprises. It processes documents, runs autonomous research workflows, and delivers structured decision support — at exactly the right moment. The name comes from the Greek concept of "kairos" — the perfect, opportune moment for decisive action.
Where is my data stored?
+
All Kiaros data is stored in Canadian data centers by default. Enterprise and Sovereign plans include a contractual guarantee of Canada-first data residency. We do not use your documents to train AI models.
Can I cancel anytime?
+
Yes. Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime with no penalty. Annual plans provide 2 months free (10 months billed) and can be cancelled before renewal. Your data is exportable at any time.
Is there a free trial for paid plans?
+
The Spark plan is free forever and gives you a real feel for Kiaros. Paid plans include a 14-day money-back guarantee — no questions asked. You can also use Interac e-Transfer (franciscoderek7@gmail.com) if Stripe isn't preferred.
