Compliance & Safety

Licensed. Regulated. Trusted.

Cannabis delivery is a privilege, not a loophole. CannaDeliver meets or exceeds every applicable federal, provincial, and municipal standard.

🎉
AGCO Licensed Delivery Partner
Authorized under the Alcohol and Gaming Commission of Ontario as a compliant cannabis delivery service. License maintained, renewed, and audited annually.
📖
Cannabis Act (Canada) Compliant
Full adherence to the Cannabis Act and its regulations. Chain of custody tracked from licensed retailer to verified recipient. No exceptions.
🏠
Municipal Bylaw Compliant
Delivery zones mapped and approved per municipal regulations. Drone flight corridors pre-approved with Transport Canada. All no-fly zones respected automatically.
👁
Age Verification at Door
Biometric face match + Government-issued ID scan required at delivery. Dual-factor verification for cannabis. Refusal and return protocol enforced automatically for all failed checks.
💉
MMAR Patient Delivery
Medical patients registered under the Marihuana for Medical Purposes Regulations receive prescription-verified, fully tracked deliveries. All MMAR documentation validated before dispatch.
🔒
Privacy — OmniaGuard Protected
All delivery data, patient records, and transaction history is encrypted end-to-end by OmniaGuard. PIPEDA compliant. Zero third-party data sharing. Your data does not leave the network.
