⚡ How Per-Job Billing Works

  • Base subscription covers your monthly platform access
  • Each job dispatched is billed at your tier rate
  • Billing calculated monthly: base + (jobs × rate)
  • Per-job invoiced manually until automated billing launches Q4 2026
  • Send job count to billing@cleanswarm.com by the 25th of each month
