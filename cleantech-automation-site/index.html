Core Platform Features

Every CleanTech Automation deployment includes the complete platform feature set — the same technology powering CleanSwarm.

🤖

AI Dispatch

Intelligent job assignment based on crew availability, location, skill set, and workload. Reduce dispatcher overhead and eliminate scheduling conflicts.

🗺

Route Optimization

Multi-stop route optimization for field crews. Reduce drive time, lower fuel costs, and increase jobs-per-day across your entire fleet.

💳

Auto-Invoicing

Automatic invoice generation on job completion. Integrated payment processing, recurring billing for maintenance contracts, and overdue reminders.

Review Collection

Automated post-service review requests. Funnel positive reviews to Google and Yelp. Flag negative feedback for internal follow-up before it goes public.
