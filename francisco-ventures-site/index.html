Investment Philosophy

Investment Thesis

Three non-negotiable pillars that define every Francisco Ventures investment.

Pillar One

Canadian Founder-First

We back Canadian operators with product-market fit, not just ideas. The founder's track record, domain expertise, and operational reality matter more than a pitch deck.

Pillar Two

Deep Technology

AI, privacy tech, regulatory compliance technology, and field service automation. We invest where technology creates durable competitive advantage — not where it is a commodity layer.

Pillar Three

Mission-Aligned

Companies that align with Francisco Holdings' core verticals. We are operators first — we invest where we can add genuine value beyond capital, and where we understand the market deeply.
