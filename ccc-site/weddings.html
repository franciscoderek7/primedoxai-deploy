What Is a Cannabis Wedding?

A cannabis wedding ceremony integrates sacramental cannabis as a sacred element of your union — acknowledging the plant's role in healing, spiritual connection, and shared intention. It is not a party trick. It is a ceremony that honours the plant, the couple, and the profound act of commitment.

Every ceremony is administered by Reverend Derek Francisco — an ordained minister with 20+ years of experience working with cannabis as medicine and sacrament. These ceremonies are Charter-protected religious practices under s.2(a) (Freedom of Religion) and comply fully with Ontario marriage law and provincial requirements.

Each ceremony includes a custom terpene profile matched to the couple's intentions — uplifting, relaxing, creative, or loving. The result is a ceremony that is deeply personal, legally registered, and spiritually meaningful.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is a cannabis wedding ceremony legal in Ontario?
Yes. Reverend Derek Francisco is an ordained minister whose authority to perform marriages is fully recognized under Ontario law. All ceremonies meet provincial requirements for legal registration. The sacramental use of cannabis in a religious context is additionally protected under Charter s.2(a) — Freedom of Religion.
Do all guests have to participate in the sacrament?
Absolutely not. Participation in the sacramental cannabis element is entirely voluntary for all guests. We are respectful of every guest's personal, medical, and spiritual preferences. Non-participating guests are honoured guests at every moment of the ceremony.
What cannabis products are used in the ceremony?
All cannabis products used in CCC ceremonies are legally obtained and Health Canada compliant. We work exclusively within the legal regulated market. Products are selected for their terpene profiles, therapeutic properties, and ceremonial appropriateness — never for novelty.
Can we choose our own strain for the ceremony?
Yes — Harvest and Empire tier packages include strain selection as part of the experience. During your consultation, Reverend Francisco will discuss the terpene characteristics of available options and help you choose a profile that matches the mood and intention of your ceremony.
Do you travel for ceremonies outside Lindsay?
Yes. Reverend Francisco regularly performs ceremonies in Lindsay, Peterborough, Kawartha Lakes, and Barrie. GTA and further destinations can be arranged — please contact us to discuss travel logistics and any additional fees. Remote/video ceremonies are also available for exceptional circumstances.
