Know Your Rights

Charter Protection

Charter s.2(a) — Freedom of Religion
Your Rights Are Protected By Law
Your right to practice your religion — including sacramental cannabis use — is protected under the Canadian Charter of Rights and Freedoms, section 2(a): Freedom of Religion and Conscience. This protection has been argued successfully in Canadian courts and forms the constitutional backbone of everything the Cannabis Church does.

The Cannabis Church provides documentation, educational frameworks, and ministerial support for members who choose to assert these rights. Reverend Francisco's own litigation history — including Francisco v. AG Canada — provides a tested foundation for these arguments that few organizations can match.
