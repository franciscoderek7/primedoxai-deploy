The Origin Story

From Patient to Advocate

In the early 2000s, Derek Francisco discovered what the medical establishment had failed to offer him — relief. Dealing with chronic pain stemming from years of demanding physical work, Derek found in cannabis a medicine that worked when nothing else did. This wasn't a casual discovery. It launched a two-decade commitment to understanding the plant, the law, and the system that controlled both. His background — spanning military and civilian service — gave him a discipline and a refusal to accept bureaucratic obstruction as a final answer.

Derek's first MMAR (Marihuana Medical Access Regulations) registration was just the beginning. He quickly realized that hundreds of patients across Canada were being denied access, confused by complex paperwork, intimidated by government processes, or simply left behind by a system that wasn't built for them. He became an expert navigator of the MMAR framework, then fought the sweeping changes brought by the MMPR (Marihuana for Medical Purposes Regulations) when the government attempted to dismantle the personal production model that protected patient sovereignty. He helped hundreds of patients access their medicine — not by working around the law, but by knowing it better than the bureaucrats enforcing it.

In 2010, Derek founded Canadian Cannabis Consulting — formalizing a practice that had already been operating through networks of patients and advocates. The years that followed were marked by regulatory battles, compliance work for emerging producers, and increasingly bold legal interventions. Derek engaged directly with the constitutional landscape of cannabis law: the Parker decision, the Allard case, the Smith ruling. He understood that the constitution — specifically sections 7, 12, and 15 of the Charter of Rights and Freedoms — was the most powerful tool any cannabis patient or producer possessed. He used it. He taught others to use it.

Now it is 2026, and CCC is reopening with more firepower than ever. Backed by ZPrimeDoxAI — 22 AI specialists trained on cannabis law, cultivation science, compliance frameworks, and business development — Derek Francisco brings the full weight of Francisco Holdings Inc. to bear on behalf of every client who walks through the door. The Superhighway is open. The guide has arrived. And the fight isn't over — it's just better equipped.
