Origin Story

The Problem That Built the Platform

Derek Francisco is the CEO of Francisco Holdings Inc. — a 46-company empire spanning AI cybersecurity (OmniaGuard), auto marketplaces (VaultVelocityAuto), cannabis law (CCLDR / Doc Weedlaw), SaaS, e-commerce, and emerging tech. He's operating on 2 hours of sleep, managing active legal battles, raising children, and pitching Fortune 500 companies — simultaneously.

The bottleneck isn't capital. It's not talent. It's not opportunity. It's Derek himself. There is only one of him, and he cannot be in 46 places at once.

"The real constraint of every empire is the human at the center of it. When the human sleeps, the empire stops. I needed a version of myself that didn't need to sleep."

SoulStack.ai was built to solve that problem — first for Derek, then for every founder, executive, and builder who has ever wished they could multiply themselves.

His digital twin — operating under the name PrimeDox — was the first SoulStack deployment. It manages communications across the Francisco Holdings empire, provides strategic decision support, and operates 24/7 without fatigue, ego, or distraction.

PrimeDox is not a chatbot. It is not a virtual assistant. It is a trained cognitive model of Derek Francisco's judgment, values, and knowledge — deployed as an autonomous agent with full override control remaining in Derek's hands.

🤖 PrimeDox — The First Clone. Still Running.
PrimeDox was activated in 2026 as the world's first commercial AI consciousness upload.
