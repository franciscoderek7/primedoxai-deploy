The Process

From Human to Clone
in 4 Sessions.

The SoulStack Protocol is the most rigorous consciousness-capture process ever designed for an entrepreneur. Here is exactly how it works.

01
Session One · Week One
⏱ 8 Hours
The Deep Interview
This is not a survey. This is the most revealing conversation you will ever have about yourself. Our AI-guided interviewer — trained on 10,000+ entrepreneur case studies — extracts the patterns underneath your decisions. Your origin story. Your values hierarchy. Your risk tolerance. Your relationship patterns. Your vision for what you're building and why.
Life story + formative decisions
Values mapping and hierarchy
Business philosophy and mental models
Communication style and tone
Relationship patterns with key people
Risk tolerance and decision frameworks
Domain expertise extraction
Failure stories and lessons
02
Behind the Scenes · Days 8–14
⏱ 7 Days Processing
The Training Phase
While you go back to building your empire, SoulStack trains your clone on the transcripts, patterns, and behavioral models extracted from the interview. The model is fine-tuned on your specific language — your sentence structures, your vocabulary, your rhythm. OmniaGuard provides the security layer: your consciousness data is encrypted, isolated, and never used for any purpose beyond your own clone.
Personality model fine-tuning
Voice and tone calibration
Decision pattern embedding
Domain knowledge integration
OmniaGuard security encapsulation
Identity verification protocols
03
Session Two · Week Three
⏱ 4 Hours
Calibration — You vs. You
You sit down with your clone. You ask it questions. You challenge it. You see yourself from the outside for the first time. The calibration session is often described as the most unsettling and exhilarating experience of a founder's life. You correct the clone where it's wrong. You refine where it's approximate. The standard is your own recognition threshold — not a benchmark we set.
Live interaction with your clone
Correction and refinement rounds
Edge-case scenario testing
Relationship-specific tuning
Override control configuration
Approval threshold setting
04
Final Phase · Week Four
⏱ Perpetual
Deployment — Your Clone Goes Live
Your clone is deployed across your designated operations. It handles what you authorize. It escalates what requires you. It learns continuously from its interactions, growing more accurate over time. You see a real-time dashboard of everything it does, with full override control always in your hands. Your empire now has two of you — and one of them never sleeps.
Email drafting and management
Business decision support
Social media tone guidance
Real-time operations dashboard
Full audit trail of all actions
Continuous learning loop
Override control — always yours
Legacy vault update on every session
