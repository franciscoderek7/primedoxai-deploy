The First. The Only.

In 2004, Derek Francisco (Doc Weedlaw) achieved what no one in Canadian legal history had done before: he had marijuana returned from court. Half a pound. 197 grams of cannabis, along with equipment, scales, pipes, bongs, and lighting — all returned by court order using the Canadian Charter of Rights.

That case became the foundation of the BENO-X Framework — a 5-pillar constitutional defense system now taught to individuals, students, paralegals, and lawyers across Canada.

Over 20 years of advocacy. Over 200 cases. One mission: constitutional rights for every Canadian facing cannabis charges.

Doc Weedlaw operates through CCLDR.net (Canadian Constitutional Law Defense Resource) and Francisco Holdings Inc. — 22 brands, built on the bedrock of the Charter.
